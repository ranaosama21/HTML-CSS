PESUDO SELECTOR

A pseudo-class is a selector that selects elements that are in a specific state, e.g. they are the first element of their type, or they are being hovered over by the mouse pointer.

TYPES OF PESUDO SELECTOR

There are two types of pseudo-selecter

  1. pseudo-class
  2. pseudo-elements

PESUDO-CLASS

  • link
  • visited
  • hover
  • active
  • first child
  • last child
  • nth child

PESUDO ELEMENTS

  • first letter
  • first line
  • before
  • after
